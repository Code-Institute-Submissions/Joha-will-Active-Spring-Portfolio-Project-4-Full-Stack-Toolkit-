We at Active-Spring

are aware that maintaining your physical fitness is crucial to leading a happy and healthy life. You may have heard that specialists advise adults to engage in 150 minutes of moderate exercise or 75 minutes of intense exercise per week. Here at Active-Spring, we follow our passions by using swimming to achieve these goals. Swimming is not only fun, but it also has many health benefits. Swimming is an excellent way to work your entire body and cardiovascular system. An hour of swimming burns almost as many calories as running, without all the impact on your bones and joints.

Indoor Pool

In our heated indoor pool, feel free to jump in and make a splash. Our indoor pool has a shallow end and a deep end, with a shallow end that is 1.2m deep and a deep end that is 3.8m deep. The pool is approximatley 100m in length, 50m in width and has a total of ten swimming lanes. Up to 120 people can use the pool at once. Each session lasts an hour, and our guests are free to book however many sessions they require. Once the pool is open, lifeguards are positioned around the pool to keep our guests safe, and there are safety warnings posted all around the pool that visitors must abide by.
